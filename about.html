About

Dr. Subhash chand is associate professor of physics at National Institute of Technology Hamirpur (HP) 177005.
He has done matric from GHS Harchakian (HP) 176210 in 1980.
He has done his intermediate from C.N.I. Boys Inter College, Dehradun in 1982.
He has done B.Sc. & M.Sc. (Physics) from D.B.S. College Dehradun.
He has done his M. Tech. (Materials Science) & Ph.D. from IIT Kanpur.
He is a member of various Professional Societies.
He has attended many conferences & seminars overseas.

Area of Specialization

Condensed Matter Physics (Experiments and Simulations), Materials Science.

Membership of societies
